Dining on the boat is a pleasure in itself, with the sea, sun and fresh air all around you to make every meal special. But add to this the elegantly served table on the Queen’s aft deck and a delicious food prepared by our experienced chef, and all ingredients are right for a truly remarkable experience.

You can have all your meals onboard or choose to go to local restaurants. Our chef will bake fresh bread every day and cook the best dishes from Turkish or international cuisine. There are no compromises in the quality of food on board the Queen of Datca. Breakfast styles, timing of the meals, your favourite fruits and diet requirements – you can expect to get what you wish. The menu will be discussed with you in advance and all your preferences will be catered for.
