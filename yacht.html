This is the official website of the owner of the Queen of Datca (pronounced as Datcha) – a classic yacht built in 2009 by Yener Yat in Turkey. The Queen was designed as a true sailing yacht in the shape of a traditional Turkish gulet, combining elegance and performance. Sailing equipment is of the highest specification, with 1000 sq. metres of sails.

The Queen of Datca is extremely well equipped to provide for your comfort. As you’d expect from a 36m gulet, there is ample deck space with a seating area and cushioned spaces, a range of water toys including a jet ski, and comfortable and spacious interiors with satellite TV. Besides, unlike most other gulets, sophisticated equipment onboard will let you enjoy the quietness of the night.

The Queen can take up to 10 passengers in 5 cabins (two master, one double and two twin that can also be converted to doubles). The 6-strong crew is committed to making your holiday a perfect one. Maltese flag and EU licence give you a complete freedom of destinations.

The Queen of Datca is the ultimate choice for relaxed cruising or sailing, if you want the best of both worlds. She is available for charter in Croatia, Greek islands and Turkey.
